About JoyDrill

Our Goal

At JoyDrill, we believe technology should do more than consume electricity — it should create joy. Our mission is simple: feed people, make them happy, and bring them together.

What We Do

JoyDrill uses your computer’s unused power to mine cryptocurrency. Instead of keeping the profits, we donate them to food banks, shelters, and programs helping those in need.

Why We Do It

Too many go hungry, too many communities remain divided. JoyDrill turns a few CPU cycles into full plates, shared smiles, and stronger connections.

Our Vision

Imagine a world where running one simple app could:
• Help stock a pantry.
• Fund a community meal.
• Spark joy in someone’s life.

That’s the world we’re drilling toward — one hash at a time.
