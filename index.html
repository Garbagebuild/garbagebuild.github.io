Welcome to Ethical Mining for Charity

What if donating to a church or homeless shelter didn’t cost you a penny? That’s the idea behind ethical mining. Instead of asking for money, we invite you to share your computer’s unused processing power to mine cryptocurrency. All of the mined currency is then donated to real-world causes: shelters, churches, and people in need.

No hidden tricks. No data collection. 100% transparent.
You can pause or stop the miner at any time. Your machine, your rules.

We’re building a movement — a way to help without spending money. With your help, we can turn idle CPUs into warm meals, working heaters, and safe beds.
